Volunteer

Give Your Time. Change a Life.

Our volunteers are the backbone of CBN. From tutoring students in Bombali to running community health drives in Pujehun, volunteers make our field programmes possible.

  • Choose from short-term, long-term or one-off opportunities
  • Roles in Freetown, provinces or fully remote support
  • Training, mentorship and a certificate of service
  • Open to Sierra Leoneans, diaspora and international volunteers

Partner With Us

Let's Build Something Together.

We work with foundations, corporates, government agencies and faith-based groups to deliver measurable impact in Sierra Leone. Our partners get quarterly reporting, on-site visits and a dedicated CBN-SL relationship manager.

  • Grant partnerships & multi-year programme funding
  • Corporate social investment & employee engagement
  • Technical collaboration & capacity sharing
  • In-kind support (equipment, transport, expertise)
