About Community Builders Network-Sierra Leone

We are a Sierra Leonean-led NGO working with communities, government and partners to build a fairer, healthier and more prosperous Sierra Leone.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

What we do, and the future we're working to create alongside the communities of Sierra Leone.

M

Our Mission

To empower women, girls, people with disabilities and the under-served population through access to education, health, agriculture, economic opportunities, leadership development and advocacy, enabling them to break barriers, claim their rights and shape a more just and equitable society that drive positive social change.

V

Our Vision

A Sierra Leone where every community has the power, opportunity and resources to lead, thrive and transform their lives..

Our Journey

History & Achievements

Nine years of milestones — from a small volunteer collective in Freetown to a nationwide community development network.

2010

CBN-SL Founded

Community Builders Network- Sierra Leone (CBN-SL) was established in 2017 by three friends united by a shared vision to address pressing social and economic challenges affecting vulnerable population across sierra leone.Across many communities, CBN-SL's founders identified deeply rooted issues, including high levels of youth unemployment, inadequate economic opportunities and the persistent under representation of women in governance at both local and international level.These challenges are further compounded by the impacts of climate change, which continue to threaten livelihoods and community resilience. In addition, poor water facilities, inadequate hygiene and sanitation systems, and the overall deplorable condition of many communities contribute significantly to poor health outcomes and reduced quality of life..Recognizing the urgent need for sustainable and inclusive interventions, the founders came together with a collective commitment to drive meaningful change. CBN-SL was therefore, created as a platform to empower communities, amplify marginalized voices, and promote development initiatives that are both impactful and long-lasting.

2018

Corona Emergency Response

Deployed 200 community health mobilizers during the outbreak, reaching more than 40,000 households with life-saving information.

2020

Girls' Education Programme

Launched the flagship scholarship scheme that has kept 1,500+ girls in school across three districts.

2024

Almost Nationwide Reach

Expanded operations to 7 districts of Sierra Leone with a network of 98 community facilitators.

2026

Recognized Nationally

Established a National Recognition for CBN-SL's continued excellent work.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Eight principles that guide every decision, project and partnership at Community Builders Network.

Integrity

We are transparent, honest and accountable to the communities we serve and the partners who trust us.

Accountability

We take responsibility for our actions, decisions and use of resources and we are answerable to our partners, beneficiaries and donors.

Inclusiveness

We work with everyone — women, youth, persons with disabilities and marginalized groups — leaving no one behind.

Transparency

We uphold openness in our operations, finances and decision-making processes to build trust with our stakeholders and the public.

Innovation

We embrace creative and adaptive solutions to address community challenges and improve the effectiveness of our interventions.

Community Participation

We recognize communities as key partners and actively involve them in the planning, implementation and evaluation of all initiatives.

Sustainability

We design programs that protect the environment and build local capacity to ensure lasting impact beyond project timelines.

Equity

We are committed to fairness and to addressing systemic disparities by ensuring resources and opportunities are distributed based on need.
